    1 Maximin

    \[Decision = Min(Worst_Future)\] \[Decision = Min({-4,-2,-3,-0.5,0\}) = 0\] \[Decision = No\ Action\]

    2 Maximax

    Choose the decision Max of Best future col which is +8 corresponding to “invest 8”

    \[Decision = Min(Best_Future)\] \[Decision = Min({+8,+4,+2,+1,0\}) = 0\] \[Decision = invest\ 8\]
