`&`{.bash}
`find -name something.txt &`{.bash}
runs find in the background


Output redirection - Overwrite
>
echo "hello" > bleh.txt
echo "hey" > bleh.txt
bleh.txt will only contain "hey"

Output redirection - Append
>>
echo "hello" > bleh.txt
echo "hey" > bleh.txt
bleh.txt will contain "hello\n hey"
